## What are embeddings?

Embeddings are numerical representations of text that capture semantic meaning. They convert words or sentences into vectors so that similar meanings have similar numerical representations.

## Use cases

Embeddings are used in search engines, recommendation systems, chatbots, and AI applications like semantic search.

## Challenges

One challenge is that embeddings require computational resources and may not always capture context perfectly. They also depend on the quality of the model used.
